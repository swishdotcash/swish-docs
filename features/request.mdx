Request USDC from anyone by generating a shareable link. The payer clicks the link, signs in, and pays — all without seeing your wallet address.

## How to request

1. Enter the amount you want to receive on the home screen
2. Tap **Receive**
3. Optionally add a message (up to 50 characters) — e.g., "Dinner last night"
4. Review the fee breakdown and the amount you'll receive
5. Tap **Proceed**
6. Copy the generated link and share it with the person you're requesting from

## What the payer sees

When someone opens your request link:

- The amount you requested
- Your optional message
- The fee breakdown
- A button to pay the request

They'll need to sign into Swish (if they aren't already) and approve the transaction. Your wallet address stays hidden throughout the process.

## Managing requests

You can view all your requests in the **Activity** tab on your profile:

- **Open** — waiting to be paid
- **Settled** — paid by someone
- **Cancelled** — you cancelled the request

To cancel an open request, click on it from the Activity tab.

## Fee breakdown

The same fees apply as with sending — charged by the [Privacy Cash](https://privacycash.org) protocol, not by Swish:

> **0.006 SOL (in USDC) + 0.35% of the amount**

The **"You Receive"** line shows the exact amount you'll get after fees. The payer covers the fees.

See [Fees](/fees) for more details.
