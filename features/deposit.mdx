---
title: "Deposit"
description: "Fund your Swish wallet with SOL or USDC."
---

To send payments through Swish, you'll need USDC in your wallet. You can deposit by sending SOL or USDC directly to your Swish wallet address.

## How to deposit

1. Go to your **Profile** (tap your avatar on the home screen)
2. Tap **Deposit**
3. You'll see:
   - A **QR code** for your wallet address
   - Your full **wallet address** with a copy button

Send SOL or USDC (on Solana) to this address from any wallet or exchange.

## What you can deposit

| Token | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **USDC** | Used for all Swish payments. This is what you need to send/receive. |
| **SOL** | Needed in small amounts for transaction fees. |

<Tip>
  If you're depositing from an exchange, make sure to select the **Solana** network when withdrawing USDC or SOL. Sending from the wrong network will result in lost funds.
</Tip>

## Checking your balance

Your balances are visible in two places:
- **Home screen** — tap "Balance" to see your USDC balance
- **Profile** — see both USDC and SOL balances with USD values
