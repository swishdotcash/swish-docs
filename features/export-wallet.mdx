---
title: "Export Wallet"
description: "Export your embedded wallet's private key to use in other Solana wallets."
---

If you signed in with X (Twitter), Swish creates an embedded Solana wallet for you. You can export this wallet's private key to import it into any other Solana wallet app.

## How to export

1. Go to your **Profile** (tap your avatar on the home screen)
2. Go to the **Wallet** tab
3. Tap **Export**
4. Follow the prompts to reveal your private key

<Warning>
  Your private key gives full access to your wallet. Never share it with anyone. Store it securely — if you lose it and lose access to your Swish account, your funds cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

## Mobile app

Wallet export is only available on the web app. If you're using the Swish mobile app, you'll be redirected to [swish.cash/p](https://swish.cash/p) to complete the export from your browser.

## Who can export?

Export is available to users who signed in with X (Twitter), since they use an embedded wallet managed by Swish. If you connected an external wallet (Seeker, Phantom, Solflare, etc.), you already have your private key in that wallet app.
