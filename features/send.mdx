Send USDC to anyone on Solana without revealing your wallet address. The recipient sees the funds in their wallet, but your address is never exposed on-chain.

## How to send

1. Enter the amount you want to send on the home screen
2. Tap **Send**
3. Choose the recipient type:
   - **Wallet** — paste or scan a Solana wallet address
   - **X Profile** — enter an X (Twitter) handle (the recipient must have a Swish account)
4. Review the fee breakdown and the amount the recipient will receive
5. Tap **Proceed** and approve the transaction in your wallet

## Sending to a wallet address

Enter a valid Solana wallet address. You can also tap the **QR scanner** icon to scan an address from a QR code.

## Sending to an X handle

Enter the recipient's X handle (without the @ symbol). Swish will look up their account and resolve it to their wallet address.


## Fee breakdown

Fees are charged by the [Privacy Cash](https://privacycash.org) protocol, not by Swish. Every send shows a clear breakdown before you confirm:

> **0.006 SOL (in USDC) + 0.35% of the amount**

The **"They Receive"** line shows the exact amount the recipient will get after fees.

See [Fees](/fees) for more details.

## After sending

Once the transaction is confirmed, you'll see:
- The amount sent
- Who it was sent to (address or X handle)
- Fee breakdown
- What they receive

The transaction will appear in your **Activity** tab on your profile page.
