title: "Withdraw"
description: "Transfer USDC out of your Swish wallet to any Solana address — gas fees sponsored."
---

Withdraw lets you move USDC from your Swish wallet to any Solana wallet address. Unlike Send, withdrawals are not routed through the privacy protocol — but the gas fee is sponsored by Swish, so it's completely free.

## How to withdraw

1. Go to your **Profile** (tap your avatar on the home screen)
2. Tap **Withdraw**
3. Enter the **destination wallet address** (or scan a QR code)
4. Enter the **amount** to withdraw (or tap **Max** to withdraw your full USDC balance)
5. Tap **Withdraw**
6. Approve the transaction in your wallet

## Fee

| Fee | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Network Fee** | Sponsored by Swish — you pay nothing |

<Note>
  Withdrawals are **not private**. The transfer is a standard USDC transfer on Solana, so both your wallet address and the destination are visible on-chain. Use this for moving funds to your own wallets.
</Note>

## After withdrawing

You'll see a confirmation with:
- The destination address
- The amount withdrawn
- Network fee: **Sponsored**

The USDC will arrive in the destination wallet within seconds.
