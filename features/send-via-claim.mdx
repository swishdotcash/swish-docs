title: "Send via Claim"
description: "Send USDC via a claimable link — no need to know the recipient's address."
---

Send via Claim lets you send USDC by generating a link and a passphrase. Share the link with anyone — they claim the funds by entering the passphrase. No wallet address exchange needed.

## How it works

1. Enter the amount on the home screen
2. Tap **Send**
3. Instead of entering a recipient, tap **Generate a claim link**
4. Optionally add a message (up to 50 characters)
5. Review the fee breakdown
6. Tap **Proceed** and approve the transaction
7. Copy the **claim link** and **passphrase**

<Warning>
  The passphrase is required to claim the funds. It is generated with the claim and never stored anywhere.
</Warning>

## Sharing the link

After creating the claim, you'll get:
- A **claim link** (e.g., `swish.cash/c/...`)
- A **passphrase** (a secret string needed to claim)

When you tap **Copy Claim Link**, both the link and passphrase are copied together. You can then share them however you'd like — DM, text, email, etc.

## How the recipient claims

1. Open the claim link
2. Sign into Swish (if not already)
3. Enter the **passphrase**
4. Tap **Proceed**
5. Funds are transferred to their wallet

## Reclaiming funds

Changed your mind? If the funds haven't been claimed yet, you can reclaim them:

1. Go to the claim link (or find it in your **Activity** tab)
2. Tap **Reclaim**
3. The funds are returned to your wallet

Once claimed by someone else, reclaim is no longer available.

## Fee breakdown

Fees are charged by the [Privacy Cash](https://privacycash.org) protocol, not by Swish:

> **0.006 SOL (in USDC) + 0.35% of the amount**

The **"They Receive"** line shows what the recipient gets after fees.

See [Fees](/fees) for more details.

## When to use Send via Claim

- You don't know the recipient's wallet address
- You want to send money without asking for an address
- You want an extra layer of security with the passphrase
