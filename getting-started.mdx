### Connecting a wallet

1. Go to [swish.cash](https://swish.cash)
2. Click **Connect Wallet**
3. Select your wallet from the list
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

### Signing in with X

1. Go to [swish.cash](https://swish.cash)
2. Click **Connect Wallet**
3. Select **Sign in with X**
4. Authorize Swish in the X login popup
5. An embedded Solana wallet is automatically created for your account

## Sign the session message

After connecting, you'll be asked to sign a one-time message to establish your session. This signature is used to derive your encryption keys for private transactions — it's not a transaction and costs nothing.

<Note>
  If you signed in with X, this step is required before you can send or receive. A prompt will appear automatically.
</Note>

## Fund your account

Before you can send payments, you'll need USDC in your wallet. You can:

- **Deposit USDC or SOL** directly to your wallet address (see [Deposit](/features/deposit))
- **Transfer from another wallet** — send USDC on Solana to your Swish wallet address

<Note>
  If you signed in with X, Swish creates an embedded wallet for you — deposit to that address. If you connected an external wallet (Seeker, Phantom, Solflare, etc.), your Swish wallet address is the same one you connected with.
</Note>

<Tip>
  You'll need a small amount of SOL for transaction fees. Most Swish transactions require only fractions of a cent in SOL.
</Tip>

## Navigate the app

- **Home screen** — Enter an amount, then tap **Send** or **Receive**
- **Profile** — View your wallet, balances, transaction history, and manage your account
- **Activity** — See all your past transactions and their status in the Activity tab on your profile
