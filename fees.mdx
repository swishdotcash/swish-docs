Private transactions (Send, Request, and Send via Claim) include a small fee charged by the [Privacy Cash](https://privacycash.org) protocol — not by Swish. Withdrawals are free.

## Fee breakdown

Every private transaction is charged by the [Privacy Cash](https://privacycash.org) protocol:

> **0.006 SOL (in USDC) + 0.35% of the amount**

- **0.006 SOL** — converted to USDC using the live SOL/USD price from the **Pyth Network**
- **0.35%** — a percentage of the transaction amount for private routing

**Example:** Sending **$100 USDC** (assuming SOL at ~$120):

| | |
|---|---|
| Base fee (0.006 SOL) | ~$0.72 USDC |
| Routing fee (0.35%) | $0.35 USDC |
| **Total Fee** | **~$1.07 USDC** |
| **They Receive** | **~$98.93 USDC** |

## Withdrawals

Withdrawals are **free**. Swish sponsors the gas fee for all withdrawals. Note that withdrawals are not private — they're standard USDC transfers on Solana.

## Where fees are shown

Every transaction modal shows a complete fee breakdown before you confirm, including the exact amount the recipient will receive after fees. You always know what you're paying before you approve.
