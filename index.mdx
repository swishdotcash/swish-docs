## Private payments, made simple

Swish is a privacy-focused payments app built on Solana. It lets you send and receive USDC without revealing your wallet address on-chain — like Venmo or Cash App, but private and on-chain.

Whether you're paying a friend, requesting money, or sharing a claimable link, Swish keeps your financial activity between you and the people you transact with.

## Why Swish?

On Solana, every transaction is fully public. When you send someone money, both wallet addresses and the amount are visible to anyone. Swish changes that.

- **Your address stays hidden** — Swish routes transactions through [Privacy Cash](https://privacycash.org) so your wallet address is never exposed on-chain.
- **No address sharing needed** — Send money via X handles or claimable links instead of asking for wallet addresses.
- **Built on Solana** — Fast, low-cost transactions.
- **USDC only** — Stable, dollar-pegged payments. No price volatility.

## How it works

1. **Sign in** with your Solana wallet (or Seeker's built-in wallet) or X (Twitter) account
2. **Enter an amount** on the home screen
3. **Choose an action** — Send, Request, or Claim
4. **Done** — your transaction is processed privately on Solana

Every transaction is routed through Swish's privacy protocol, which ensures your wallet address is never linked to the payment on-chain.
